About Me

Hi! I'm Mikaela Miranda, a second-year Computer Engineering student at Cal State Long Beach and an LSAMP Research Fellow. I'm passionate about designing accessible assistive technology, with a focus on cochlear implants and hearing science. My work bridges biomedical engineering and machine learning to improve surgical planning and patient education. Currently, I'm developing a path-planning algorithm and validating it using physical models I designed and printed myself. I'm driven by my own experiences with hearing devices and a strong desire to make innovation more inclusive and impactful.
